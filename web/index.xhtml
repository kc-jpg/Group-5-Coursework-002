Welcome to the World of Formula 1

Formula 1 is the highest class of international motorsport sanctioned by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). Known as the pinnacle of motorsport, F1 races take place on purpose-built circuits and street circuits across the globe, attracting millions of fans worldwide.


Each season consists of a series of races known as Grands Prix, held across multiple continents. Teams compete using highly advanced single-seater cars powered by hybrid turbocharged engines, pushing the boundaries of engineering and technology. Drivers battle not only for race victories but also for the coveted World Drivers Championship and Constructors Championship titles.


With legendary circuits like Monza, Silverstone, and Monaco, and iconic champions such as Ayrton Senna, Michael Schumacher, and Lewis Hamilton, Formula 1 has a rich history spanning over 70 years of racing excellence.
